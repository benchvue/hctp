About Us

Hanaim Community Tutorial Program (HCTP) is offered by Hanaim Church, a non-denominational church located in Elmwood Park, NJ.

“Hanaim”, with its roots in the Bible, means togetherness in Korean. We believe that all of us need to learn to live together — embracing, respecting and valuing others as they are, without judging them based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, or any other characteristics.

HCTP is part of our effort to serve the communities that we live in, particularly children, who are our future.
